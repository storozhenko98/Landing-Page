"Thus far, photographers have strived to capture with a camera as if it were a receptacle of rays of light, not unlike our own eyes. I treat the camera differently. I approach photography in the same way that the Ancient Greeks approached vision--the notion that vision is an activity whereby the eye sends out a ray and thereby illuminates what is before it. In a sense, I apporach photography in the same way that phenomenologists approach consciousness--I shine a spotlight on to the world and hence by illuminate, for me, the camera is the projector"
-Mykyta Storozhenko
