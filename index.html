About Me

My name is Mykyta Storozhenko and I was born in Crimea in 1998. I came to the United States in 2009 and started studying philosophy in College. I graduated in 2018 with a B.A. in Philosophy from Florida Atlantic University. Along with a passion for philosophy, there I found my passion for photography. Afterward, I completed an M.A. program at Kent State Univeristy, where, for the first time, upon taking Graduate Level Logic, I became interested in programming. Currently, I am a PhD student at the University of Kentucky, working on a philosophy dissertation as well as my software development skills.
